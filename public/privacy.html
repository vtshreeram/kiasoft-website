We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
